Dungeons and Dorks

Roll Your Dice

Welcome, brave adventurers and curious wanderers, to the mystical realm of Dungeons and Dorks! If you’re wondering what the name means, let’s just say it’s a place where saving throws and self-deprecating jokes go hand-in-hand, where dragons are as likely to crack a pun as they are to breathe fire, and where the dice are fickle enough to turn a fearless hero into a clumsy dork in the blink of a natural one.

Our intrepid gang, known as the Rusty Saw, is a motley crew of misfits and mirth-makers. At the helm is Joe King, our bard, and self-proclaimed leader in the spotlight. He’ll serenade you with a ballad one moment and then trip over his own shoelaces the next, much to our amusement.

However, the real brains behind this outfit is Peren, our wizard. He’s the one who keeps the magical mishaps at bay, or at least tries to, between bouts of snark and sarcasm. You’ll find his quips as sharp as his spellbook, which is saying something.

The dynamic duo of wood elf clerics, well, let’s just say they’re a handful. These two are always bickering over who heals better, and one of them has an obsession with crossbows that could rival an armory.

Lastly, we have Henk, the lovable half-orc who is a bit too heavy for his flying broomstick and has an undying love for smashing in doors. He’s the muscle of the group, and believe me, he doesn’t knock; he obliterates.

So, grab your dice and your sense of humor, and join us in the land where the only thing sharper than our swords is our wit. Dungeons and Dorks – where adventure meets absurdity, and the only critical hit is a punchline!
